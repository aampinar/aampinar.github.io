About Me


What's your Background?

Hello, my name is Ampinario Ash and I am currently a Commercial Dealer Service Analyst at Ford Motor Credit. I enjoy working with dealer personnel and the Commercial sales team to put offerings together and make the customer financing experience as smooth and seamless as possible. Previous to my time at Ford, I was a certified Personal Trainer with clients ranging from high school/college athletes to regular folks with goals of body sculpting, energy enhancement and increased functional body mechanics.

Why Do You Want to Be a Software Engineer?

To me, software engineering is a wonderful way for people to create and build solutions for modern problems and a powerful form of self-expression.
Most of my career has been customer-centric with the goal of improving customer experience, whether that be with a Ford vehicle or a personalized fitness plan. I recognize that my reach is limited with human-to-human interactions and I feel that software engineering has the scale, where I can make the most impact. I look forward to nurturing my engineering skills to further enhance the customer experience and exploring the infinite possibilities of software engineering.
